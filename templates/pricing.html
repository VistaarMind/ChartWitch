Frequently Asked Questions

Can I change plans later?
Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect at the next billing cycle.
How are API requests counted?
Each time you use a ChartWitch feature like signout notes, cleanup, or discharge summaries, it counts as one API request.
Is there a free trial?
New users can try ChartWitch with 10 free API requests. After that, you'll need to subscribe to a plan to continue using the service.
How do I cancel my subscription?
You can cancel your subscription anytime from your account page. You'll continue to have access until the end of your current billing period.
Is my data secure?
Yes, ChartWitch is fully HIPAA compliant with advanced encryption for all patient data. We adhere to strict security protocols and never store sensitive patient information.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards and debit cards through our secure payment processor, Stripe.
