About Us

ChartWitch is a simple tool created to harness the power of Artificial Intelligence to solve the everyday frustrations of modern medical charting and tackle the ever-expanding cognitive burden of medical practice. Our goal is to help clinicians make the most of their EMRs and give back time & energy to focus on patient care and improve their work-life balance.

Our Vision

To transform clinical workflows with intelligent, secure, and user-friendly technology that restores joy to medicine and reduces burnout.

Our Mission

To empower healthcare workers by harnessing the power of automation to improve various aspects of clinical workflow - including documentation, comprehension, analysis, communication & more...
